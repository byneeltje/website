Exquisite Corpse - all is connected
Hello imaginative people,

This is an invitation to create an Exquisite Corpse together. This is the luxurious name of a game you've probably played before! One draws "the head", another "the body" and a third "the base" and then ... a magical creature comes to life!

Every Sunday in April I will send you an A4 PDF like the one posted here with a number of markings and three words. You can print this PDF and use it as a basis or you can copy the markings yourself onto an A4 format sheet. 

In order to play together, it is important to use the same size paper, but the color, quality or material does not matter. As long as you can send it by post ...

Steps: 
  • Fold your sheet in three sections and draw "the head" in the top part, inspired by the first word.
  • Fold the top section backwards, so that you only see the two bottom parts with the marking where you have to start and end.
  • Then draw the second part, inspired by the 2nd word, or, if you have house mates, pass the sheet on to them.
  • Repeat this procedure for the third part, inspired by the third word.

You get it, this will not just be an Exquisite Corpse but one that consists of absolutely everything that comes to your mind. Maybe you draw a teapot as a head, an armadillo body and a bicycle as legs! Everything is allowed and all is good. After all, our world is not only made up of people and animals !

Play this game as many times as you like. I have one request though: Please send me  1 or 2 of your creatures every week by post, folded or not folded, what works best for you. Or deposit it in our postbox if you live in Cascais and are going to get some fresh air anyway.

Digitally but preferably by post: I love to receive post! Also I would like to create something tangible with it, a way to depict the connection between everything and everyone. Despite and thanks to Covid-19.

I wish you health, lots of love and fun,
Neeltje​

Why these beauties ? 
The strict lockdown prevents us from our normal exercise so we decided to finally have a go with the surf-skateboards that have been sole decoration in our apartment until now.

Lucky us. Not only do we love how the board revives the child in us and helps us through our midlife doubts, it also takes us to the park and its beautiful diverse canine community.

So here I am, every other day if the weather allows. Boarding and - during my break - stalking a beauty for a photo shoot.
